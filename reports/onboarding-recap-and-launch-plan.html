05

How we'll measure success

The right metric at the right time
Days 0–14
Contact Rate & Pacing
Are you reaching the leads? Is the $75 daily budget pacing cleanly across the day? Earliest indicator the campaign is healthy.
Days 15–30
Quote Rate
Of the leads you contact, how many become quote-worthy? Source-level patterns emerge here — we start tuning bids and channels.
Days 30–60
First CPA Read
First reliable Cost Per Acquired Policy. Calibrate bids against the actual close data and start narrowing zips.
Days 60–90
ROI & Scale
Sustained CPA + revenue trend. Decide what to scale, what to optimize further, when to layer in the deferred campaigns.
