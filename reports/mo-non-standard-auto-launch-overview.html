Recommendations

1
Confirm the go-live date
The campaign is fully configured and activates the same day the date is confirmed.
2
Ad creative updates before launch
We are updating the ad to non-standard messaging — no-current-coverage, SR-22 filing, and a call prompt — so the ad reaches the shoppers the campaign is priced for.
3
Respond to new leads within 5 minutes
Contact rates fall sharply after the first few minutes. Uninsured shoppers typically purchase the same day; a text after a missed call raises callback rates.
4
Export disposition data ahead of each review
Contact, quote, and bind statuses from AgencyZoom feed the bid adjustments by channel and shopper profile at each biweekly review.
