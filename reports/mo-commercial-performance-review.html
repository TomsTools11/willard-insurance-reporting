Raise the Email source bid from 100% to 105%

3
Reduce the Premium Referral bid from 90% to 75%
The current $11.26 bid is 36% above the source’s $8.28 first-position market price, and 2 of its 15 leads and calls have been contacted to date. The new $9.38 bid still clears first position by 13%; the difference funds the increases above.
4
Hold Benepath - Social and the base bid unchanged
Benepath - Social stays at 115% ($14.39): it is 6% above its $13.56 first-position price with a 48.48% win rate and 2 policies bound. All updates above run through source multipliers; the $12.51 base bid is unchanged.
5
Review bid results and budget pacing on June 17
The daily budget stays at $75 with the 7-per-day lead cap. Spend through June 10 is $849.29. If daily spend reaches the budget cap before the lead cap after these updates, we raise the daily budget to $90.
