The Missouri Commercial campaign launched May 22. Through July 7 it produced 172 leads and calls from 214 ad clicks at an average ad position of 1.83. We are adjusting source bids to current first-position market pricing and holding the base bid; details are in section 04.

2
Reduce the Premium Referral bid from 90% to 85%
The current $11.26 bid is 6.9% above the source’s $10.54 first-position market price. Premium Referral holds 361 of the campaign’s qualified opportunities and 62 of 172 leads and calls (36%), with 2 of 57 leads contacted to date. The new $10.63 bid still clears first position; the difference funds the increases above.
3
Hold Benepath - Google Search and Benepath - Social unchanged
Benepath - Google Search stays at 120% ($15.01), at its $15.23 first-position price with a 37.1% win rate and the highest bound volume. Benepath - Social stays at 115% ($14.39), 1.3% above its $14.20 first-position price with a 59.2% win rate. The $12.51 base bid is unchanged; all updates run through source multipliers.
4
Hold Email at 100% and review source relevance
Email holds 1,302 of the campaign’s qualified opportunities and won 1.1% of its auctions at a $12.51 bid against a $13.73 first-position price. We are holding the bid and reviewing source relevance before adding spend.
5
Budget pacing
The daily budget is $75 and reached its cap on July 1, 2, and 6. Spend since launch is $2,787.99. A daily budget increase to $90 is available to expand volume.
